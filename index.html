On the next screen, you’ll be able to search through a diverse list of wearable devices using keywords and feature filters. Once you’ve added at least two devices to the comparison menu, you’ll be able to compare the devices with relevant features side by side? Not enough features? Click to add features that you care about to the comparison. Too many? Click to remove features to make comparisons easier.
